About the Book

She agreed to a
marriage of ruin.
She didn't agree to love him.

Seraphine Voss has spent her life outrunning her family's debts. When the ruthless and untouchable Dorian Ashveil offers to clear every last one — in exchange for her hand — she tells herself it's a transaction. Nothing more.

But Dorian is a man who collects beautiful things and destroys them slowly. And Seraphine is starting to wonder whether she is the exception to his cruelty — or simply his most patient conquest.

Set in a fog-drenched city between worlds, Crimson Vows is a novel about obsession, power, and the terrifying intimacy of being truly known by someone who should be your enemy.

⚠ Content Warning: Dark themes, possessive hero, morally grey characters

An Excerpt

"You look at me like I'm something dangerous," Dorian said, his voice low — not threatening, which was somehow worse.

"Aren't you?"

He was quiet for a long moment. The fire crackled between us, and I watched the light move across his face, searching for something — a tell, a flinch, anything human.

"To most people," he said finally. "Not to you."

The Author
✍

Judimae Trapa

Author of Dark Romance

Judimae Trapa writes stories about love as a force of nature — beautiful, indiscriminate, and occasionally devastating. Her prose is known for its lush atmosphere, emotionally complex characters, and slow-burning tension that refuses to let go.

Crimson Vows is her latest novel, a story born from a fascination with power, desire, and the fine line between surrender and strength.

She is currently working on her next dark romance. She promises it will hurt — beautifully.
